Avengers: Endgame

About the Movie: Avengers: Endgame is a 2019 American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics superhero team the Avengers. It is the direct sequel to 2018's Avengers: Infinity War and the fourth and final installment of the Avengers series in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). Directed by Anthony and Joe Russo, the film follows the Avengers as they attempt to undo the damage caused by Thanos in the previous film.

After Thanos's snap wiped out half of all life in the universe, the surviving Avengers must band together and use the Quantum Realm to travel back in time to collect the Infinity Stones before Thanos can use them to erase the Avengers once and for all. With breathtaking action sequences and emotional depth, Endgame provides a climactic conclusion to over a decade of storytelling in the MCU.

Genres: Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi

Directed by: Anthony Russo, Joe Russo

Release Year: 2019
