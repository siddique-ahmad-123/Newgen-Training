The God Must Be Crazy

About the Movie: The God Must Be Crazy is an educational IMAX nature documentary narrated by Kristen Bell that focuses on the conservation and protection of giant pandas. It highlights the efforts of scientists in China and the United States to reintroduce captive-bred pandas into the wild, showing both the challenges and successes of the program.

This film explores the beauty of the panda's natural habitat, the struggles the bears face, and the fascinating behaviors they exhibit in their efforts to survive in the wild. Through stunning visuals and a touching narrative, the documentary showcases the dedication of individuals working to save these majestic creatures.

Genres: Nature, Documentary, Wildlife

Directed by: Richard Ladkani

Release Year: 2018
