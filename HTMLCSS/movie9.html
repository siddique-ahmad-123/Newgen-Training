About the Movie: The Nun is a 2018 supernatural horror film directed by Corin Hardy. It is part of The Conjuring Universe and serves as a prequel to the main series, focusing on the demon Valak, who is first introduced in "The Conjuring 2." The film follows a young nun named Sister Irene (played by Taissa Farmiga) and a priest named Father Burke (played by Demian Bichir), who are sent to investigate the death of a nun at a secluded convent in Romania. As they uncover the dark secrets of the convent, they encounter terrifying supernatural forces.

The movie explores the origins of Valak and the haunting presence that terrorizes the characters. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they discover that the entity is far more malevolent and dangerous than they imagined.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Directed by: Corin Hardy

Release Year: 2018
