The Conjuring 2

About the Movie: The Conjuring 2 is a 2016 American supernatural horror film directed by James Wan, and is a sequel to 2013's The Conjuring. The film is based on the true events of the Enfield poltergeist case in 1977, which involved a haunting in a suburban London home. The Warrens, played by Patrick Wilson and Vera Farmiga, return to help a family plagued by supernatural events.

The movie focuses on the terrifying presence of a malevolent spirit tormenting the Hodgson family in the UK. With disturbing paranormal activity and chilling encounters, the film creates a tense atmosphere as the Warrens attempt to uncover the truth behind the haunting and confront the powerful demon.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Thriller

Directed by: James Wan

Release Year: 2016
