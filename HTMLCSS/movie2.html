Home Alone

About the Movie: Home Alone is a classic Christmas comedy film directed by Chris Columbus, written by John Hughes, and starring Macaulay Culkin as Kevin McCallister. It follows the story of an 8-year-old boy who is accidentally left home alone during Christmas vacation and must defend his house from two burglars, played by Joe Pesci and Daniel Stern.

This heartwarming and hilarious film has become a holiday staple, known for its slapstick humor, memorable quotes, and the young Kevin's clever traps to outwit the burglars. It's a film that has captured the hearts of audiences for decades and is a must-watch for families during the holiday season.

Genres: Comedy, Family, Christmas

Directed by: Chris Columbus

Release Year: 1990
